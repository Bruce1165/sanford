## 🎯 Quick Reference

### Project Objective
Build a predictive system that detects coffee cup pattern formation **before completion** by combining signals from 11 technical analysis screeners.

### Current Status
- **Phase**: Phase 1 - Research & Analysis
- **Progress**: 5%
- **Data Status**: Historical screener data generated (1,856 runs completed)
- **Blocker**: ✅ Resolved

### Key Metrics (Targets)
- **Precision**: ≥ 60%
- **Recall**: ≥ 50%
- **F1 Score**: ≥ 0.55
- **Lead Time**: ≥ 10 days

### Timeline
- **Total Duration**: 10-18 weeks (5 phases)
- **Current Week**: Week 1 of 2 (Phase 1)
